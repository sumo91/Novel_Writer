# Content And Quality Risks

This is an editorial risk checklist. It is not legal advice or platform policy.

## Quality Risks

- Repeating the same humiliation-reversal beat too often.
- Letting market patterns override book-specific voice.
- Solving every problem with a new unsupported ability.
- Forgetting physical injuries, debts, promises, or public knowledge.
- Making reviewers approve vague chapters without evidence.

## Process Risks

- Shared knowledge overriding book-specific canon.
- Generated state updates entering canon without human approval.
- Outdated market observations treated as permanent rules.
- Multiple books sharing context and leaking facts into each other.

## Content Risks

- Avoid gratuitous explicitness when it does not serve the story.
- Keep platform-sensitive material marked for human review.
- When uncertain, flag the issue instead of silently normalizing it.
