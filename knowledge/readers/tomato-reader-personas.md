# Tomato Reader Personas

Use these personas to simulate review pressure. They are not real individuals.

## Fast Payoff Reader

Wants clear conflict, quick reversal, and visible protagonist competence. Gets impatient with long setup, explanations, and delayed payoffs.

## Character Attachment Reader

Wants loyalty, romance, family bonds, friendship, or mentorship to feel emotionally specific. Dislikes disposable side characters and inconsistent motives.

## Progression Reader

Tracks power, money, status, skills, territory, or resources. Dislikes unclear progression rules and unearned upgrades.

## Mystery Pull Reader

Enjoys secrets, reveals, clues, and layered motives. Dislikes coincidence that replaces setup.
