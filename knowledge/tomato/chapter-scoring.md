# Chapter Scoring Rubric

Use this rubric for first-pass Tomato pacing review. Scores are guides for revision, not automatic publication decisions.

| Dimension | Points | Review Question |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Hook strength | 10 | Does the opening create pressure or curiosity quickly? |
| Conflict clarity | 10 | Is the central chapter conflict easy to understand? |
| Protagonist agency | 10 | Does the protagonist actively choose or act? |
| Emotional payoff | 15 | Does the chapter reward the reader's expectation? |
| Pacing | 10 | Does the chapter move without dead explanation? |
| Character consistency | 10 | Do characters act from known motives and states? |
| Continuity safety | 10 | Does it respect known canon, timeline, and facts? |
| Chapter-end pull | 10 | Does the ending create a reason to continue? |
| Mainline relevance | 10 | Does it advance the core arc or a tracked thread? |
| Fresh information or expectation | 5 | Does it add a new useful fact, promise, or complication? |

Suggested thresholds:

- 90-100: strong candidate.
- 80-89: usable after light revision.
- 65-79: revise before acceptance.
- Below 65: rebuild the chapter brief or core conflict.
