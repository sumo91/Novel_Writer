# Opening Hook Patterns

Use these patterns to generate options, not to force every book into the same shape.

## Situation Hooks

- Public humiliation with a hidden reversal.
- A deadline that will ruin the protagonist if missed.
- A betrayal that exposes a bigger system.
- A family crisis that forces action.
- A low-status protagonist sees an impossible opportunity.
- A secret identity is about to be exposed.

## Power Hooks

- A dormant talent activates under pressure.
- A system or inheritance appears with a cost.
- A past-life memory changes a present decision.
- A professional skill becomes unexpectedly decisive.

## Emotional Hooks

- Protecting someone who cannot protect themselves.
- Refusing a humiliating deal.
- Choosing revenge at a cost.
- Saving an enemy for strategic reasons.
- Discovering love, loyalty, or family was built on a lie.
