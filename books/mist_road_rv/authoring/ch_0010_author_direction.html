Chapter 10 Author Direction

chapter: 10
author_intent:
- Preserve chapter 10 as the first-station closure and Qinghe transition chapter.
  The point is not a new battle win, but proving that the RV can continue under rules,
  low filter stability, damaged sealing, and medical pressure.
- Keep the opening pressure immediate: broadcast, rear-seal damage, fog-wall countdown,
    low-efficiency filter, and Zhou Xiaohe's condition before any Qinghe explanation.
- Make Lu Chenzhou's payoff a boundary procedure: he does not blindly rescue or coldly
    abandon; he verifies the call, keeps the vehicle pointed toward retreat, refuses
    door-opening pressure, and chooses a controlled route to the medical-point exterior.
- Make Lin Zhixia's stay feel functional rather than sentimental. She becomes responsible
  for medical observation, pollution triage, and the isolation zone because she already
  acts like a crew member.
- Keep Zhou Xiaohe as a temporary observation patient whose injury justifies the medical-line
  pressure, not as a formal teammate or a tool for emotional blackmail.
- Let the ending open Qinghe with concrete threats: medical-point material signal,
    abnormal door access, rising pollution, and possible voice mimicry.
must_change:
- Do not unlock the Simple Medical Bay in chapter 10.
- Do not fully repair the clean filter or rear seal.
- Do not enter the Qinghe main building or reveal the full Qinghe survivor order.
- Do not open the RV door to unverified outsiders.
- Do not let the system make moral choices for Lu Chenzhou; it can only report route,
  risk, status, and module clues.
- Do not promote Zhou Xiaohe to formal crew or living-space access.
- Remove any meta-fictional wording; all dialogue must stay inside character and world.
